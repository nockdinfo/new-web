Shipping Policy

At NockD, we aim to deliver your orders swiftly and safely. Once your order is placed, our team carefully processes and dispatches it within 1–3 business days. Delivery timelines vary based on your location, typically taking 3–7 working days for most regions across India.

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your order arrives on time and in perfect condition. You will receive tracking details once your order has been shipped, allowing you to monitor its progress easily.

Shipping charges may apply depending on your order value, delivery location, or during special promotions. All such details will be clearly mentioned during checkout before you confirm your purchase.

If your package arrives damaged or you experience any delivery issues, please contact our support team within 48 hours of receiving the product. We’ll assist you with replacements or solutions as per our return policy.
