Privacy Policy

At NockD, your privacy matters to us. We’re committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe, transparent shopping experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit or make a purchase on our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information – such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping details when you create an account or place an order.
  • Payment Information – handled securely through trusted payment gateways; we do not store your full payment details.
  • Technical Data – such as IP address, browser type, and device information to improve website performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing orders, payments, and deliveries
  • Sending order updates and customer support messages
  • Improving our website, services, and product offerings
  • Personalizing your experience and recommending relevant products

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your information is stored securely and used only for legitimate business purposes.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may only be shared with trusted third-party service providers (like payment processors or shipping partners) who help us operate our business — and only to the extent necessary.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly without them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To make such a request, please contact us at support@nockd.in.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the updated date mentioned below.
